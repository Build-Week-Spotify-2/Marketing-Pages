How does it work?

Our song recomendations are based off of sound attributes that your known favorite songs share. Things like accousticness; dancibility, energy, instrumentalness, key, loudness, speechiness and more, are all things we use to create a list of recomended songs you might want to add to your own spotify playlists, based off of how much of these musical attributes are associated with your own known likes.
